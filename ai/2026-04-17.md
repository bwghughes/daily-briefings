## 🏆 Biggest Story Today

**Anthropic releases Claude Opus 4.7 — retakes LLM crown**

Anthropic dropped **Claude Opus 4.7** yesterday, describing it as a "notable improvement" over Opus 4.6 in advanced software engineering. Early benchmarks suggest it narrowly reclaims the top spot on general availability leaderboards from GPT-5.4. Key additions: new `thinking_effort: xhigh` mode via the API, improved `thinking_display` and `thinking_adaptive` options, and increased default `max_tokens`. The model is now live for all tiers. Simon Willison already ran head-to-head pelican benchmarks against Qwen3.6-35B-A3B — the Qwen model held its own, with some preferring the Chinese model's SVG output.

→ [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-7) | [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/anthropic-releases-claude-opus-4-7-narrowly-retaking-lead-for-most-powerful-generally-available-llm) | [Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Apr/16/qwen-beats-opus/)
