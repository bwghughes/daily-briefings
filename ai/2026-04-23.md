## 🍎 Apple Silicon & Local AI

**Apple ML Research at ICLR 2026 — 12 Papers**
Apple's ML team is presenting 12 papers at ICLR in Rio de Janeiro (April 22–26). Covers code generation, on-device models, Apple Silicon optimization, and ANE utilization. Notable follow-up from April 2026: "Embarrassingly Simple Self-Distillation Improves Code Generation" (arXiv:2604.01193).
→ [Apple ML Research](https://machinelearning.apple.com/research/iclr-2026)

**Orion Paper: ANE for LLM Training, Not Just Inference** *(ANNE Innovation Watch)*
First major academic reverse-engineering of Apple's Neural Engine showing it can be used for LLM *training*, not just inference. From Ramchand Kumaresan. GitHub: mechramc/Orion (57 stars). This is significant — officially ANE only supports inference via CoreML; Orion proves training workloads are achievable.
→ [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06728v1) | [GitHub](https://github.com/mechramc/Orion)

**NeuralForge: On-Device LLM Fine-Tuning on ANE**
Reverse-engineered tool enabling LoRA fine-tuning directly on Apple Neural Engine via private APIs. Signaled as part of the "agent wars" momentum — training is officially off-limits on ANE, community working around it.
→ [Agent Wars](https://agent-wars.com/news/2026-03-13-neuralforge-on-device-llm-fine-tuning-on-mac-using-apple-neural-engine) | [GitHub](https://github.com/Khaeldur/NeuralForge)

**ANE LoRA Training Repo Active** *(early signal)*

**maderix/ANE — 6,500+ Stars, Largest ANE Reverse Engineering Effort**
