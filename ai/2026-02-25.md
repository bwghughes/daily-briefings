# 🤖 AI Morning Briefing — Wednesday 25 February 2026

## 🔴 Biggest Story: Anthropic Goes Full Enterprise

Anthropic launched a sweeping set of job-specific plugins for Claude Cowork targeting finance, HR, legal, banking, and research departments. The platform now integrates with Google Drive, Gmail, DocuSign, FactSet, Excel and PowerPoint — essentially turning Claude into a departmental agent, not just a chatbot. Markets liked it: Salesforce +4%, FactSet +5%, DocuSign +6%. This comes hot on the heels of Anthropic's "legal plugin" that triggered a wider market rout a few weeks ago. ## 🍎 Apple Silicon & Local AI

**Qwen 3.5 is the story of the week on r/LocalLLaMA.** Qwen3.5-35B-A3B dropped — sparse MoE, 397B total params, only 17B active per pass. Benchmarks claim it beats DeepSeek-R1, Grok 3, and Gemini 2.5 Pro on several tasks. Recommended to run with **VLLM-MLX** on Apple Silicon.

**MLX is pulling ahead of llama.cpp.** Community consensus: LM Studio's MLX backend wins on memory efficiency and tokens/sec. Q4_K_M is the sweet spot — sub-4-bit quants are actually *slower* on Apple Silicon due to dequant overhead. Qwen3-Coder-30B-A3B running at **49 t/s with 120k context** on an M1 Max 32GB.

**M4 Mac Mini 64GB = reference local AI rig.** Multiple community benchmarks dropping this week for 30B-class inference. M4 Pro's unified memory architecture getting serious attention as real inference hardware.

**vLLM-MLX** (launched Jan 2026) — 464 tok/s on Llama 3.2 1B on M4 Max, 197x real-time Whisper STT, full multimodal, OpenAI-compatible API.

**oMLX** (launched ~2 weeks ago) — "Drag to Applications, done" MLX inference server with SSD-paged KV caching. Solves the coding agent context problem. Multi-model serving (LLM + Embedding + Reranker simultaneously).

**Distributed inference via Thunderbolt 5 RDMA** — pooling multiple Mac Minis described as "the most interesting development in local LLM hardware since Apple Silicon unified memory." Still maturing.

**Privacy angle:** Apple Silicon is the undisputed community favourite for private, local AI. "Why send data to a cloud when your Mac Mini handles 30B locally?" going mainstream as enterprise AI gets more invasive.
