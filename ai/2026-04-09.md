## 🚨 Top Story: Anthropic's Claude Mythos — Too Dangerous to Release

Anthropic announced **Claude Mythos Preview** yesterday — a frontier model so powerful at finding security vulnerabilities that they're **restricting access** to a limited group under **Project Glasswing**, a $100M initiative to patch the world's software before the model becomes widely available.

**Key Stats:**
- **93.9% on SWE-bench Verified**, 94.6% on GPQA Diamond
- Found **thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities** across every major OS and browser
- Partners: Apple, Amazon, Broadcom, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorgan, Linux Foundation, Microsoft, Nvidia, Palo Alto Networks

**Why It Matters:** This is the first major frontier model to be **withheld from public release** due to cybersecurity concerns. Mythos' ability to autonomously find critical vulnerabilities represents a "reckoning" moment for software security. The model is roughly two months ahead of Opus 4.6 in capability.

## 🏆 Major Model Releases

### GLM-5.1: The 8-Hour Autonomous Developer

Chinese startup Z.ai (Zhupai AI) released **GLM-5.1**, a 754B-parameter MoE model under **MIT license**, designed to work autonomously for up to **8 hours** on a single task.

**Benchmarks:**
- **58.4 on SWE-Bench Pro** (beats GPT-5.4 at 57.7, Opus 4.6 at 57.3, Gemini 3.1 Pro at 54.2)
- **3.6x speedup on KernelBench** (vs 2.6x for GLM-5, 4.2x for Opus 4.6)
- Built a functional Linux-style desktop environment from scratch in one 8-hour run

**Architecture:** 754B params, 202K token context, "staircase pattern" optimization that identifies structural bottlenecks autonomously rather than hitting plateaus.

**Pricing:** $1.40/M input, $4.40/M output via [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/z-ai/glm-5.1) and [Requesty](https://www.requesty.ai/models/zai/glm-5-1)

**Why It Matters:** First model to productively use **1,700+ tool-call sequences** without strategy drift. Community feedback highlights week-long tasks being completed in two days.

### Meta Llama 4: Scout & Maverick

Meta released **Llama 4 Scout** and **Llama 4 Maverick** on April 5, 2025 — the first native multimodal MoE open-source models.

**Models:**
- **Scout:** 17B active params (109B total), 16 experts, **10M context window** (largest available)
- **Maverick:** 17B active params (400B+ total), 128 experts, 1M context

**Pricing:**
- Scout: $0.17/M tokens, 128 tokens/sec
- Maverick: Higher throughput, enterprise-grade

**Why It Matters:** Llama 4 Scout offers the **longest context window** of any production model (10M tokens), designed for long-document processing and analysis.

### Google Gemma 4: First Official iPhone Local Model App

Google launched **Gemma 4** on April 2 with four variants (E2B, E4B, 26B-A4B, 31B), switching to **Apache 2.0 license** for full commercial use. The **AI Edge Gallery app** now allows iPhone and Android users to run Gemma 4 **locally and offline**.

**Key Features:**
- Multi-step planning & agentic workflows
- 140+ language support
- Runs on-device via **LiteRT-LM**
- Text, image analysis, transcription, automation

**Why It Matters:** This is the **first time a major local model vendor has released an official iOS app** for trying models on iPhone. Conversations are ephemeral (no permanent logs yet), but it's a major step for on-device AI.
