## 🍎 Apple Silicon & Local AI

### ANE Innovation Watch

**Orion Paper (arXiv:2603.06728) — ANE Now Confirmed Usable for Training**  
Researchers have published the first open end-to-end system for training on Apple Neural Engine. Orion bypasses CoreML entirely via Apple's private _ANEClient and _ANECompiler APIs, cataloguing 20 ANE restrictions (14 previously undocumented). Achieves 170+ tok/s GPT-2 124M inference on M4 Max. Training recompilation reduced from 4,200ms to 494ms per step — an 8.5x speedup. Stable 110M parameter transformer training demonstrated in 22 minutes with zero NaN occurrences. Also introduces LoRA adapter-as-input via IOSurface inputs, enabling hot-swap without recompilation. [arXiv:2603.06728](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06728v1) | [GitHub: mechramc/Orion](https://github.com/mechramc/Orion)

**maderix/ANE Repo — Training on ANE via Reverse-Engineering**  
The pioneering repo enabling neural network training on ANE via reverse-engineered private APIs. 6,539 stars, 921 forks. [GitHub: maderix/ANE](https://github.com/maderix/ANE)
