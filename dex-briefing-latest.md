## 🔴 Biggest Story: Apple Business Launches — Free Built-in MDM & Zero-Touch Deployment

Apple's new **Apple Business** platform launched April 14, unifying Apple Business Manager, Business Essentials, and Business Connect into a single free service available in 200+ countries. Key DEX-relevant features:

- **Free built-in MDM** for device management — no paid subscription required for core management
- **Zero-touch deployment** for Apple devices purchased through Apple or authorized resellers
- **Blueprints** for preconfigured device setup (settings + apps) ensuring consistency out of the box
- **Managed Apple Accounts** with cryptographic work/personal data separation
- **Identity integration** with Google Workspace and Microsoft Entra ID for automated account creation
- **Admin API** for large-scale deployment automation

This is a significant shift — Apple is essentially offering enterprise-grade device management for free, directly competing with Microsoft Intune, Jamf, and other UEM vendors. IT teams managing Apple fleets now have a viable free path for core MDM and zero-touch workflows.

🔗 [Apple Newsroom announcement](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2026/03/introducing-apple-business-a-new-all-in-one-platform-for-businesses-of-all-sizes/) | [9to5Mac breakdown](https://9to5mac.com/2026/04/18/apple-work-free-apple-device-management-is-a-baseline-not-a-finish-line/)

### Nexthink — DEX Goes Mobile (Android + iOS)
Nexthink launched **Mobile Experience**, extending its DEX platform to Android and iOS. The pitch: UEM tools manage config and compliance, but don't answer *"can mobile users actually do their jobs?"* The new mobile module provides:
- Device performance monitoring (memory, storage, battery health trends)
- Continuous Wi-Fi/cellular network tracking (distinguishing device-side vs infrastructure issues)
- Compliance/security posture visibility (OS patches, encryption)
- App usage and AI app adoption insights
- Data-driven hardware refresh decisions

Nexthink is explicitly going after the blind spot for frontline/mobile workers where the primary device isn't a laptop.

## 📊 Market Landscape & Trends

- **Agentic AI in DEX**: Riverbed's Aternity Self-Service, ControlUp's Autonomous Endpoint Management pivot, ServiceNow's full AI integration — the shift from reactive monitoring to proactive/autonomous resolution is accelerating across the market
- **Full-fidelity visibility**: The competitive moat is no longer just device metrics — vendors that can correlate device + app + network data in one platform (Riverbed, Omnissa, Nexthink) are winning against single-domain players
- **Mobile DEX goes mainstream**: Nexthink's mobile expansion signals that smartphone/tablet as primary work device is becoming a first-class DEX concern, especially for frontline/mobile workers
- **Shadow AI is the new shadow IT**: Omnissa's ~1000% growth in AI assistant usage puts shadow AI firmly on IT's radar — DEX tools increasingly need to provide visibility and governance here
- **Free MDM pressure**: Apple Business's free tier puts pricing pressure on third-party UEM vendors — organizations may consolidate to Apple's free tier for Apple-only fleets
- **Sustainability enters DEX**: HP's Carbon Footprint Report signals sustainability metrics are becoming part of the digital employee experience conversation
